Make sure both devices are on the same WiFi network 3. Open the app on your Mac first (it will appear in your menu bar) 4. Open the app on your iPhone 5. Tap your Mac's name to connect 6. Start moving your cursor! --- Common Issues Can't see my Mac in the iPhone app? - Ensure both devices are on the same WiFi network - Check that the Mac app is running (look for the mouse icon in your menu bar) - Try closing and reopening both apps - Restart your WiFi router if needed Connection keeps dropping? - Move closer to your WiFi router - Check that your Mac isn't going to sleep - Ensure "Low Power Mode" is off on your iPhone - Try switching between WiFi and Bluetooth in network settings Cursor movement is too fast/slow? - Adjust sensitivity in Settings (coming soon) - Try switching between Gyro Mode and Trackpad Mode - Make sure you're in a well-lit room for Gyro Mode Which mode should I use? - Gyro Mode: Best for presentations and pointing from across the room (hold phone upright) - Trackpad Mode: Best for precise control and close-range use (hold phone horizontally) --- System Requirements iPhone/iPad: - iOS 18.0 or later - iPhone 6s or newer Mac: - macOS 15.0 or later - Apple Silicon or Intel processor --- Troubleshooting Reset Connection: 1.
